Cancellation & Refund Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Cancellation Policy

We understand that circumstances can change. Our cancellation policy is designed to be fair to both our clients and our team.

Custom Website Projects

  • Projects can be cancelled within 7 days of project initiation
  • Cancellation after work has commenced will result in charges for work completed
  • All cancellations must be submitted in writing to help.grock@gmail.com
  • Cancellation requests are processed within 2-3 business days

Hosting Services

  • Monthly hosting plans can be cancelled with 30 days notice
  • Annual hosting plans can be cancelled with 60 days notice
  • Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period
  • No cancellation fees for hosting services

2. Refund Policy

Our refund policy varies depending on the type of service and timing of the request.

Custom Website Development

  • 100% refund if cancelled within 7 days and no work has started
  • Partial refund based on work completed if cancelled after project initiation
  • No refund for completed and delivered projects
  • Refund requests must be submitted within 30 days of payment

Hosting Services

  • Pro-rated refund for unused hosting period (monthly plans)
  • Annual plans: Refund for unused months minus setup fees
  • Setup fees and domain registration costs are non-refundable
  • 30-day money-back guarantee for new hosting accounts

3. Refund Process

To request a refund, please follow these steps:

  1. Send an email to help.grock@gmail.com with your refund request
  2. Include your order number and reason for refund
  3. Provide your payment details for refund processing
  4. Wait for confirmation and processing (5-7 business days)

4. Non-Refundable Items

The following items are non-refundable:

  • Domain name registration fees
  • Third-party software licenses
  • SSL certificates
  • Setup and configuration fees
  • Custom add-ons that have been implemented
  • Projects completed and delivered as per specifications
