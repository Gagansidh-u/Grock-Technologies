Shipping & Delivery Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Service Delivery Overview

Grock Technologies provides digital services, which means our "delivery" refers to the completion and handover of digital products and services. We do not ship physical products unless specifically mentioned in your service agreement.

2. Digital Service Delivery

Our digital services are delivered through the following methods:

Website Development

  • Live website deployment on your chosen hosting platform
  • Source code delivery via secure file transfer
  • Documentation and user guides via email
  • Access credentials provided securely

Hosting Services

  • Account setup within 24 hours of payment confirmation
  • Login credentials sent via email
  • Control panel access provided immediately
  • DNS configuration instructions included

3. Delivery Timelines

Standard delivery times for our services:

Custom Website Development

  • Simple website (5-10 pages): 7-14 business days
  • Medium website (10-20 pages): 14-21 business days
  • Complex website (20+ pages): 21-35 business days
  • E-commerce website: 21-42 business days

Hosting Services

  • Shared hosting: Immediate activation
  • VPS hosting: 2-4 hours setup time
  • Dedicated hosting: 24-48 hours setup time
  • Domain registration: 1-2 hours
